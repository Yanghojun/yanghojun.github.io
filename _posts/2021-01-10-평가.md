# 기본 지식
## Norm

- $\vert\vert W \vert\vert_p = (\sum_{i=1}^p \vert W_i \vert ^p)^{1 \over p}$
- $L_2$ norm  예시
    
    $\vert \vert W \vert\vert_2 = \sqrt{W_1^2 + W_2^2} = \sqrt{W^TW}$
